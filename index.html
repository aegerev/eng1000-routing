About

Everything needs a foundation. Without it, everything crumbles, everything falls apart.
Imagine a house. Without the proper foundation, without the proper support, that house will fall.
The same is true for education. If there is no foundation, there’s no success.

You can try out every single program to speak English, but the truth is that not all programs will give you the foundation needed to succeed. That's where we come in.
The "Learn English with Me!" program changes everything. This is the program where we will be working on building the foundation for you to succeed in learning or advancing your English, which will help you succeed in life! I am invested in your success, and I am here to help you.
It will be hard at first, but it will be well worth it.

With the help of Lingolia, a website that is tailored to give you an “extra layer” of foundation that will help you master English, through stories, conversations, and of course, homework, you'll be well on your way to learning English.
So, if you're ready to take your English to the next level, please consider booking a trial class with me!

I hope to see you soon!
